Rule

1. pick an item in your inventory and click hit.

2. when you hit, you use a stone. blackstone armor for reblath < 15, concentrated blackstone armor for reblath > 15, concentrated weapon for dande > 15.

3. when it succeeds, item's level will get +1 and failstack becomes 0.

3. when it fails, item's level will get -1 (if the level is above 16), durability decreases. and you get failstack.

4. failstack increases the chance to succeed.

5. when item durability is 0, you need to repair it. repairing reblath uses reblath (1 for 10 dura) and dande uses memory fragment (1 for 1 dura).

6. when reblath hits level 15, it automatically downgrades to level 14. (success rate for level 14 is lower than level 15 so it is good to build failstack)

7. reblath is cheap, dande is expensive. try to make dande 19 or 20 by building high stack and gain profit
